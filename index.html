About Us

Hotel Life Inn.

Hotel Life Inn is a welcoming lodging establishment nestled in the vibrant Bani Park area of Jaipur, Rajasthan. Conveniently located on Jhotwara Road opposite Chamatkareshwar Mandir and near Space Cinema, we offer a peaceful retreat for families, couples, and travelers exploring the Pink City. With a stellar rating of 4.4 out of 5 from over 263 guests, our commitment to quality and comfort speaks for itself.

At Hotel Life Inn, we pride ourselves on our courteous and attentive staff who go above and beyond to make every stay memorable. From immaculately maintained rooms to a kitchen that serves truly satisfying meals, we ensure that your experience with us is worth every rupee. Whether you're visiting Jaipur for leisure, pilgrimage, or business, Hotel Life Inn is your ideal base in the city.
